## Answer

## Question 9
### Prove:  P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)
## Answer
Let A,B two event   
P(A-B) means part of A not overlap with B ; P(B-A) means part of B not overlap with A  
P(A)=P(A-B)+P(A &#8745; B) ; P(B)=P(B-A)+P(B &#8745; A)  
P(A ∪ B)=P(A-B)+P(B-A)+P(A &#8745; B)  
=>P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B)
